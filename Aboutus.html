About US

Lanka PC is a leading ICT product seller and service provider in Sri Lanka, catering to the needs of SMEs, state institutions, and large enterprises. With a strong presence since its establishment in 2000, Lanka PC has built a reputation for providing cutting-edge and reliable technology solutions to its diverse clientele.
